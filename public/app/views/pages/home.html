About CSNet

CSNet was created to solve the problem of information overload in the department, specifically in two areas: too many different intranet-type sites that all performed similar functions (see the list above), and too much departmental email being sent to the various CS group email lists.

Github

This last problem was particularly annoying to students, so the News & Announcements application was envisioned by Dr. Taylor as a mechanism to disseminate departmental information in a less intrusive manner. This is a new venture for us, so any feedback you may have, either reporting a bug you have found, or in terms of improving the user interface, is greatly appreciated.
